About
Shree Valliamman
Thirumana Mahal

Shree Valliamman Temple Mahal located at the foothills of marudhamalai is one among the most magnificent marriage halls in Coimbatore. It is Managed by the managing trustee of Shree Valliamman Temple a charitable organization.With the best state of the art facilities and known for its cultural feel, built with serenity and prosperity. Some of the landmarks like Vadavalli Bus terminus, Bharathiyar University, Coimbatore Law College, Anna University are surrounded by the Mahal.
